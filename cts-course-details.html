Course Details

The Craft Instructor Training Scheme is operational since inception of the Craftsmen Training Scheme. This is a competency based course for instructors of one year duration.This course consists of four sub-courses according to choice of trainee and these are Computer Hardware & Networking Maintenance,Computer Software Application,Electronics Mechanics,Electrician & Wireman.

The main objective of Crafts Instructor training programme is to enable Instructors explore different aspects of the techniques in pedagogy and transferring of hands-on skills so as to develop a pool of skilled manpower for industries, also leading to their career growth & benefiting society at large. Thus promoting a holistic learning experience where trainee acquires specialized knowledge, skills & develops attitude towards learning & contributing in vocational training ecosystem.

Duration and Syllabus of the course

Under Craftmen Training Scheme (CTS) we offer three trades Solar Technician (Electrician), IoT (Smart Agriculture), IoT (Smart Healthcare) each of two semesters and have distributed syllabus.

  • SOLAR TECHNICIAN (ELECTRICIAN)

    Syllabus

    • In first year the trainee will learn about all Electronic and Electric stuffs including Solar related information much more.

    Assessment

    • The Continuous Assessment (Internal) during the period of training will be done by Formative Assessment Method by testing for assessment criteria listed against learning outcomes.
    • The final assessment will be in the form of summative assessment. The All India Trade Test for awarding NTC will be conducted by Controller of examinations, DGT as per the guidelines
  • IoT TECHNICIAN (SMART AGRICULTURE)

    Syllabus

    • Under this course the trainee wil learn about the Electrical/electronic measurement by selecting of single range with safety precautions , about analog circuits , troubleshooting and other related things

    Assessment

    • The Continuous Assessment (Internal) during the period of training will be done by Formative Assessment Method by testing for assessment criteria listed against learning outcomes.
    • The final assessment will be in the form of summative assessment. The All India Trade Test for awarding NTC will be conducted by Controller of examinations, DGT as per the guidelines
  • IoT TECHNICIAN (SMART HEALTHCARE)

    Syllabus

    • In first year the trainee will Perform electrical/ electronic, basic Networking, troubleshooting, Demonstrate and deploy responsive Web Application using APIs, Identify and install the devices used in green house tasks consists of professional skills and knowledge and much more.

    Assessment

    • The Continuous Assessment (Internal) during the period of training will be done by Formative Assessment Method by testing for assessment criteria listed against learning outcomes.
    • The final assessment will be in the form of summative assessment. The All India Trade Test for awarding NTC will be conducted by Controller of examinations, DGT as per the guidelines
