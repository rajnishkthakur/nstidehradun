History and Awards

National Skill Trainers Institute (NSTI) , Dehradun was established in 1982 with the motto to produce skilled workforce in India.

  • 21 May 1982

    Establishment Day

  • 24 Dec 2010

    ISO Qualified

    This Institute has been awarded with ISO 29990:2010 certificates for its excellent infrastructure and training methodology.

    Infrastructure

    It is located on a sprawling 17-acre land with well-designed workshops / laboratories equipped with state-of-the-art machinery and gadget training as the latest industry trends. In this institute, canteen and hostel services with modern amenities are also available.

    Accessibility

    NSTI is easily accessible as it is situated in the eco-friendly environment at Green Park, Niranjanpur, Dehradun nearer to the main sabji mandi, Dehradun. The Institute is approximately 25 km from Dehradun Airport, 5 km from Dehradun Railway Station and 2 km from Dehradun ISBT.

    Quality Teaching

    At NSTI, there is a healthy, harmonious Industry – Institute relationship to produce products of international quality of learning process.

  • 14 Feb 2015

    Facilities and Management

    More modern facilities and amenities ahs been added to the institute to make it more classy. Solar water heater, quality drinking water etc. has been provided.

    Student Facilities

    NSTI Dehradun has a very decent Boys Hostel accommodation for 120 male trainees that is provided on sharing basis for short term and long term courses.

    Management Facilities

    The Institute is managed by the different levels of Officers vis-a-vis DGT, Director, ADT, Training Officers, Hostel Suprintendents, Wardens and Guards.

    Education Facilities

    The role of the institute is building capacity and strengthen the existing system to meet challenges related to industry and in turn fulfill its mandate.

    Transport Facilities

    The Institute has its own staffs vehicle which helps staffs from each section to get the need of Institute fulfilled by travelling.

    Alumini Facilities

    Alumini meet are arranged annually by the students with the institute support and a samll party is being organized to serve them betterly for institute.
